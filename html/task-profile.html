Project

Design of HX on EES software

ABSTRACT

The heat exchanger is one of the most efficient devices which is used to transfer heat from one fluid to another fluid without fluid contact. There are several types of heat exchangers from which the most commonly used heat exchanger in an industrial application is shell and tube heat exchanger. It has a heat exchanging range from -20 °C to 500°C. Shell, tubes, and baffles are the main components of the shell and tube heat exchanger. The objective of the study is to design it for the cooling of hot fluid by using cold fluid with a square layout. In this the four types of fluid like ammonia, butene, ethanol & water with taking cold fluid the same as water. All calculation of the heat exchanger is done on the EES (Engineering equation solver) software which give an accurate value of all physical dimension of the heat exchanger, thermodynamic properties of a fluid by mathematical calculation without fabrication. It is possible in EES software to change all physical as well as a thermodynamic calculation by only changing the name and properties of that fluid on EES. The effect of changing baffle spacing is also studied. Baffles are used to increase the velocity of the fluid result in changing the heat transfer performance of the heat exchanger. Baffle spacing is one of the important parameters in shell and tube design.

Conclusion

This report demonstrates successful application of genetic algorithm for the optimal design of shell-and-tube heat exchangers. Approximate design method for shell-and-tube have been investigated and generalized procedure has been developed to run the GA algorithm and to find the global minimum heat exchanger area. A case study has been made for examination of the performance of genetic algorithm. From this study we conclude that the combinatorial algorithms such as genetic algorithm provide significant improvement in the optimal design compared to the traditional designs. It concludes that it is easy to design heat exchanger and other devices on EES software and the main advantage of EES software is that it gives accurate reading of all physical and thermodynamic properties of the fluid hence the safe design is possible. It reduces the human effort on designing procedure and also reduce cost by providing accurate value without Fabrication hence there is no loss of scrap material. The main advantage is that it gives values for all fluids by only changing name and properties in EES software therefore no need of extra calculation for every fluid.
